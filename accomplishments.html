Accomplishments

A few cool things I've had the good fortune of doing

Winner, Sustainability Track

Hack for Colorado 2013

Our team of three web devs, an iOS dev, and a designer built a Rails app and an iPhone app to try to increase the availability of fresh, healthy food across the country. Grubb.io is a marketplace that connects producers and consumers of locally sourced food. It allows producers to locate and expand their businesses through zoning and demographic information and it allows consumers to source their meals from local farmers.

Out of almost 100 developers and 18 teams, we took home a $2,000 prize for winning the sustainability track.

Invited to the White House

Champions of Change Event, July 2013

One of about 100 developers invited to the White House to meet with government and private sector technologists to discuss civic hacking, open data, and open gevernment. Met with representatives from federal government, NASA, Intel, and Yahoo to brainstorm on strategies for making government data work in new and creative ways.
